About Nomadic

Nomadic Hiking Club was founded in 2013 by couple Tuvshintugs Baatar and Zagdaa Baatar in Bay Area. It attracted many of the Mongolian community hikers and allowed them to connect in a unique way The club is open to anybody who wants to bring out their inner animal!
